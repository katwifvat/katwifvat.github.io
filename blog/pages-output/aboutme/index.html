About Me

Hi, my name is Kat. I'm the founder & CEO of Snappyhow, a platform for sharing tutorials, starting with Clojure for web development. I recently finished my PhD in Applied Math at Arizona State. At ASU, I was a teaching assistant for 5 years and launched my first venture which was a marketplace for teachers to sell course material. Before that, I double majored in Statistics and Applied Math at the University of St. Thomas in Minnesota, where I was born and raised. I now live in Silicon Valley.
